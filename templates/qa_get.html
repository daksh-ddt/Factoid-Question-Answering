Deep(ish)QA

Deep(ish)QA is a factoid-question-answering web application written in Python.

What is a factoid question?

Factoid questions are answered with a single fact, which usually is the name of an entity or a specific value. Deep(ish)QA can respond to questions that are answered by the names of people, cities, dates, and more than 50 other categories.
